Slack

  1. You will receive an invite from me from your Missouri State, or preferred, e-mail address. Follow the instructions to setup your account.
  2. You can use Slack in a browser at msuwebdesign.slack.com
  3. You can use Slack by downloading and installing the Mac or Windows apps
  4. You can use Slack on your phone or tablet by downloading the apps from the App Store or Google Play

Code School

  1. Go to www.codeschool.com/students
  2. Enter in your Missouri State e-mail address.
  3. Check your Missouri State e-mail for your student discount invite.

Thimble

  1. Go to Thimble's Website
  2. Click "Create an Account" in the top-right corner.
  3. Fill in required information.

That's it! When you work on an exercise in Thimble, you will be able to "Publish" it, and send me the link via Slack.

InVision

  1. Go to Invision's Sign-Up Page
  2. Fill out required information
  3. After logging in to your new account, hit the pink + and create a new board.
  4. Name it “DES 340 Your Name”
  5. Find the “Share” button, it will create a link, copy it and send me that URL via Slack.
